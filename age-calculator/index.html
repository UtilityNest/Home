Age Calculator

Age Calculator helps you calculate your exact age in years, months, and days from your date of birth. It is useful for government forms, job applications, exams, and eligibility verification.

How Age Is Calculated

This tool compares your date of birth with the selected date and calculates the difference accurately, considering leap years and calendar variations.
