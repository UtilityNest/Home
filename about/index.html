About UtilityNest

UtilityNest is a free online platform providing accurate, fast and easy-to-use calculators and utility tools for users in India.

Our tools are designed especially for government job aspirants, students, salaried employees, and professionals who need reliable calculations for exams, forms, and financial planning.

What We Offer

  • Government exam calculators (Age, Retirement, Gratuity, etc.)
  • Finance calculators (EMI, SIP, FD, RD, Salary)
  • Hindi language tools (Word Counter, Typing Tool)
  • Privacy-focused tools like Aadhaar Masking

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide simple, transparent and trustworthy tools that work instantly without login, ads overload or data collection.
